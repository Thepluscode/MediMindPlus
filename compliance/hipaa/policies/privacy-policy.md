# HIPAA Privacy Policy - MediMind Platform

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Privacy Policy establishes MediMind's commitment to protecting the privacy of Protected Health Information (PHI) in accordance with the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act (HIPAA) Privacy Rule.

### 1.1 Scope
This policy applies to:
- All MediMind workforce members (employees, contractors, volunteers)
- All systems and applications that create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI
- All business associates who handle PHI on behalf of MediMind
- All physical and electronic PHI under MediMind's control

### 1.2 Definitions
- **PHI**: Individually identifiable health information transmitted or maintained in any form
- **Workforce**: Employees, volunteers, trainees, contractors, and other persons under direct control
- **Business Associate**: Person or entity that performs functions involving PHI on behalf of MediMind
- **Minimum Necessary**: Least amount of PHI necessary to accomplish the intended purpose

## 3. Uses and Disclosures of PHI

### 3.1 Permitted Uses Without Authorization

#### Treatment
- Providing, coordinating, or managing healthcare services
- Consultation between healthcare providers
- Referrals between healthcare providers

#### Payment
- Billing and collection activities
- Claims management and processing
- Medical necessity determinations

#### Healthcare Operations
- Quality assessment and improvement activities
- Case management and care coordination
- Healthcare fraud and abuse detection
- Business planning and development

### 3.2 Uses Requiring Authorization
- Marketing communications (with exceptions)
- Sale of PHI (with exceptions)
- Psychotherapy notes (with exceptions)
- Research not covered by waiver
- Any use not specifically permitted by law

### 3.3 Required Disclosures
- To individuals upon request for their own PHI
- To HHS for compliance investigations
- As required by other applicable laws

## 4. Individual Rights

### 4.1 Right to Access
Individuals have the right to:
- Inspect and obtain copies of their PHI
- Request access in specific formats when readily producible
- Receive response within 30 days (60 days with extension)

#### Access Request Process
1. Submit written request to Privacy Officer
2. Verify identity of requestor
3. Locate and review requested records
4. Provide access or denial with explanation
5. Document request and response

### 4.2 Right to Amendment
Individuals may request amendments to their PHI when:
- Information is believed to be inaccurate or incomplete
- Request is submitted in writing with reason for amendment
- Amendment relates to PHI maintained by MediMind

#### Amendment Process
1. Review request for completeness and validity
2. Accept amendment or provide written denial
3. If accepted, make amendment and notify relevant parties
4. If denied, allow individual to submit statement of disagreement

### 4.3 Right to Accounting of Disclosures
Individuals may request accounting of PHI disclosures for:
- Six years prior to request date
- Disclosures not for treatment, payment, or operations
- Disclosures not authorized by individual

#### Accounting Requirements
- Date of disclosure
- Name and address of recipient
- Brief description of PHI disclosed
- Brief statement of purpose

### 4.4 Right to Request Restrictions
Individuals may request restrictions on:
- Use or disclosure of PHI for treatment, payment, or operations
- Disclosure to family members or others involved in care
- Specific types of PHI

#### Restriction Considerations
- MediMind is not required to agree to all restrictions
- Must agree to restrictions on disclosures to health plans for services paid out-of-pocket
- Document all restriction requests and decisions

### 4.5 Right to Confidential Communications
Individuals may request to receive PHI through:
- Alternative means of communication
- Alternative locations
- Reasonable requests must be accommodated

## 5. Minimum Necessary Standard

### 5.1 General Principle
Use, disclose, or request only the minimum amount of PHI necessary to accomplish the intended purpose.

### 5.2 Implementation Requirements
- Identify persons or classes of persons who need access to PHI
- Identify categories of PHI needed for each person or class
- Implement procedures to limit access to identified categories
- Review and update access determinations periodically

### 5.3 Exceptions to Minimum Necessary
- Disclosures to healthcare providers for treatment
- Uses or disclosures authorized by individual
- Disclosures required by law
- Uses or disclosures for compliance activities

## 6. Safeguards and Security

### 6.1 Administrative Safeguards
- Assign security responsibilities to workforce members
- Implement procedures for granting access to PHI
- Establish workforce training and access management procedures
- Implement information access management procedures

### 6.2 Physical Safeguards
- Control physical access to facilities and workstations
- Implement workstation use restrictions
- Control access to electronic media containing PHI
- Implement procedures for media reuse and disposal

### 6.3 Technical Safeguards
- Implement access control procedures for electronic PHI
- Assign unique user identification and authentication
- Implement automatic logoff and encryption procedures
- Maintain audit logs of PHI access and modifications

## 7. Business Associate Management

### 7.1 Business Associate Agreements (BAAs)
Required for all business associates who:
- Create, receive, maintain, or transmit PHI on behalf of MediMind
- Perform functions or activities involving PHI
- Provide services where PHI disclosure is necessary

### 7.2 BAA Requirements
- Permitted and required uses and disclosures of PHI
- Prohibition on unauthorized use or disclosure
- Requirement to implement appropriate safeguards
- Requirement to report security incidents and breaches
- Return or destruction of PHI upon contract termination

## 8. Complaints and Violations

### 8.1 Complaint Process
Individuals may file complaints regarding:
- Privacy practices and procedures
- Alleged violations of privacy rights
- Denial of access to PHI
- Failure to provide required notices

#### Complaint Procedure
1. Submit complaint in writing to Privacy Officer
2. Include description of privacy concern
3. Investigation within 30 days of receipt
4. Document complaint and resolution
5. No retaliation against complainants

### 8.2 Violation Response
- Immediate investigation of reported violations
- Corrective action and disciplinary measures as appropriate
- Documentation of violations and responses
- Reporting to appropriate authorities when required

### 9.2 Training Schedule
