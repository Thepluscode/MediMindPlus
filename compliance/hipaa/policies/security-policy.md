# HIPAA Security Policy - MediMind Platform

## 1. Purpose and Scope

This Security Policy establishes MediMind's framework for protecting Electronic Protected Health Information (ePHI) in accordance with the HIPAA Security Rule.

### 1.1 Scope
This policy applies to:
- All electronic systems that create, receive, maintain, or transmit ePHI
- All workforce members with access to ePHI
- All business associates handling ePHI
- All physical locations where ePHI is accessed or stored

### 4.1 Access Control (Required)

#### Unique User Identification
- Unique user identifiers for each workforce member
- User account provisioning and deprovisioning
- Account monitoring and maintenance
- Shared account prohibition

#### Emergency Access Procedures
- Break-glass access procedures for emergencies
- Emergency access monitoring and logging
- Emergency access review and validation
- Emergency access termination procedures

#### Automatic Logoff
- Automatic session termination after inactivity
- Configurable timeout periods based on risk
- Session management and monitoring
- Forced logoff capabilities

#### Encryption and Decryption
- Encryption of ePHI at rest and in transit
- Strong encryption algorithms and key management
- Encryption key rotation and lifecycle management
- Decryption access controls and monitoring

### 4.2 Audit Controls (Required)
