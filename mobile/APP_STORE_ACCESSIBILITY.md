## Customer Support - Accessibility FAQs

**Q: Does MediMindPlus work with VoiceOver?**
A: Yes! Every screen and feature in MediMindPlus is fully compatible with VoiceOver on iOS. All buttons, forms, and content are properly labeled and navigable.

**Q: Can I use TalkBack on Android?**
A: Absolutely. MediMindPlus has complete TalkBack support on Android devices running version 8.0 or higher.

**Q: Do you support Switch Control?**
A: Yes, all functionality is accessible via Switch Control and other alternative input methods.

**Q: Will my AI analysis results work with a screen reader?**
A: Yes! All medical findings have text descriptions. Critical findings are announced with high priority, so you'll hear important results immediately.

**Q: Do forms work with VoiceOver?**
A: Yes, all forms have proper labels and provide real-time feedback. You'll hear validation errors as soon as they occur, not just when you submit.

**Q: Can I adjust text size?**
A: Yes, MediMindPlus respects your system font size settings (Dynamic Type on iOS, Font Scaling on Android).

**Q: Does it work in high contrast mode?**
A: Yes, and our default contrast already exceeds WCAG AA standards (4.5:1 ratio for body text).

**Q: I found an accessibility issue. How do I report it?**
A: Please email accessibility@medimindplus.com with details. We respond within 2 business days and take all reports seriously.
