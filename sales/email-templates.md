## Email Best Practices

### Timing
- **Best days:** Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Best times:** 8-10am (before clinic starts) or 6-8pm (after clinic)
- **Avoid:** Monday mornings, Friday afternoons, weekends

### Structure
- **Subject line:** <50 characters, specific, benefit-focused
- **Opening:** Personalized (mention their practice/research/common connection)
- **Body:** <150 words, scan-friendly, 1-2 short paragraphs
- **CTA:** Single, clear ask with calendar link
- **Signature:** Professional, include phone

### Personalization
- Mention their PACS system specifically
- Reference their practice size/specialty
- Cite mutual connections
- Note any recent publications/achievements

### Follow-up Cadence
- Email 1: Day 0
- Email 2: Day 3
- Email 3: Day 6
- Email 4: Day 10
- Email 5: Day 15 (break-up)

After 5 emails with no response, stop. Add to "nurture" list for quarterly check-ins.

---

**Pro Tip:** The money is in the follow-up. 80% of sales happen after the 5th touchpoint, but 44% of salespeople give up after the first email.

Be persistent, but polite. You're offering value ($100 gift card + potentially life-saving AI), not spamming.
